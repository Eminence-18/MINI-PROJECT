FOXES

There are two species of foxes in Kenya: the bat-eared fox and the silver fox. Foxes are small to medium-sized animals that belong to the dog family. They are omnivores, feeding on small mammals, birds, and insects. Foxes are known for their intelligence and cunning nature.

The silver fox is a melanistic form of the red fox. It has black fur and is considered a rare species. The silver fox can be found in the forests of Kenya, where it feeds on small mammals, birds, and insects. The IUCN lists the silver fox as least concern and as having a stable population, though they are threatened by habitat loss and hunting.
